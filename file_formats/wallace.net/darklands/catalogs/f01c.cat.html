f01c.cat

Battle sprites and overlays for a male fighter ("Gunther" from the default party).

There are three types of animation files in here, denoted by the fourth and fifth letters of the filenames: "CB" for combat, "WK" for walking, "DY" for dying (or dead). The first two have many variants, depending on what weapon the character is wielding.
